AudienceIQ — Intelligent Content Greenlight Engine

An AI-powered platform that helps creative executives make data-backed greenlight decisions — predicting audience reception, market fit, and franchise potential before production begins.

✦ Original Concept

The Problem Worth Solving

Creative decisions are still largely made on gut, legacy relationships, and trailing data. The result: expensive productions that miss their audience — not because the story was bad, but because the intelligence wasn't there at the decision point.

AudienceIQ brings predictive AI into the earliest creative conversation, giving decision-makers a living map of audience appetite, competitive whitespace, and cultural timing.

Audience Intelligence Content Strategy Predictive AI Market Fit

How It Would Work

1
Ingest Signal

Social sentiment, streaming behaviour, search trends & demographic shifts across 50+ markets in real time.

2
Score the Concept

Audience Fit Score, Genre Saturation Index, and Cultural Timing Window — produced by a trained model against the pitch.

3
Simulate & Recommend

Release windows, cast archetypes, localisation priorities, franchise potential — all generated as strategic options.

4
Executive Brief

One-page greenlight summary built for a decision-maker's 10-minute window.
